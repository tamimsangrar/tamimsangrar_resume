What is SoftPOS?

Introduction

  • SoftPOS is a mobile first, mobile only acceptance solution that enables merchants to transform their existing smartphones into Point of Sale terminals with contactless payments acceptance capabilities.
  • It provides a cost-effective solution for all micro-merchants and SMEs in India and eliminates the need to invest in a traditional PoS device.
  • SoftPOS works on all NFC enabled Android smartphones running Android v6.0 and above.
  • It is available as a white labeled and licensable solution to acquiring entities.

features

Application Features

Bharat QR

The application supports both – Dynamic & Static Bharat QR v4.0 which includes support for merchant specific UPI VPA’s. A customer can pay using any BQR 4.0 compliant bank / wallet app as well as all UPI applications.

Cash Register

A convenient way for the merchant to keep a record of all collections across digital modes and cash. This ensures a 360 degree visibility of collections to the merchant.

Khaata

A customer wise credit ledger can be created to track all dues, send reminders & also facilitate collection against the dues using any of the payment modes made available on the application.

SMS Link

The merchant can accept payments remotely by sending payment links to customers over SMS/E-mail. The payment confirmation is updated on the merchants mobile in real time on successful payment completion.

Dukan

Merchants can create a virtual store front and catalogue for their inventory making it easier for customers to make online purchases and pay using the online payment gateway.

Report

The merchant has access to a reports section to view transaction details and also to initiate Void & Refunds.

Merchant

Merchant Onboarding

  • The merchant on-boarding experience is entirely DIY in nature across downloading, installing, registering and usage of the application.
  • The architecture is designed to facilitate an instant on-boarding and activation experience.
  • The registration flow kicks off with an OTP validation on the entered mobile number.
  • During registration, e-KYC is facilitated via instant PAN card validation using a trusted 3rd party service provider.
  • Additionally, the merchant is required to submit scanned copies of KYC documents and accept a digital T&C which can be utilized for further, deferred risk checks.

Merchant

Value added services

  • To round off the offering for the merchants, SoftPOS provides various Value Added Services integrated within the application itself.
  • The VAS offerings will cover a wide range of services from the Dukan offering to providing GST filing facilities using a trusted 3rd party service provider.
  • The VAS bouquet will be expanded over time to provide more services which offer the merchant additional revenue streams. For e.g., recharges and bill payments, etc.

Merchant

Offers

  • The Offers tab has a carousel arrangement of cards which can be used to showcase offers to merchants.
  • Each card can be linked to a specified URL which show cases the offer details. This could be an offer landing page, lead generation form, etc.
