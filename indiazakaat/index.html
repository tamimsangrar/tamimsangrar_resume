ABOUT US

Our VISION is To transform Zakat taker of today into Zakat giver of tomorrow

MISSION

  • To create a centralized national level digital platform for effective utilization of Zakat
  • To create user-friendly, secured, trusted and transparent platform to govern Zakat Management system
  • To bring the Zakat givers and Zakat takers on one platform with real time statistics
  • Under the guidance of Ulemas and Islamic finance experts, to enable Zakat as a tool for social transformation

WHY?

  • Ineffective utilization of transformational potential of divine economic tool, “Zakat”
  • Poor streamlining of Zakat distribution channels leading to unequal access to Zakat
  • No centralized database of beneficiaries and donors leading to duplicity in distribution
  • Inaccessibility of online community to donate to their respective geographies from anywhere in the world and unorganized intermediators network

HOW?

  • By creating “IndiaZakat.com”, a platform to perform Zakat calculation and donation at fingertips
  • By bringing non-profits and social leaders to create the campaigns and businessman and professionals to donate towards them
  • By governing the platform under supervision of experts around the globe
  • By utilizing AMP India’s strong network of 100+ cities, 20+ Countries and 1 Lac+ professional to perform due diligence and impact analysis
