    Hello, and welcome! I'm Tamim, and I'm excited that you've stopped by to learn more about me.

    I wear many hats in life - a strategic Product Manager, a fervent soccer enthusiast, an avid gamer, and even a proud, new plant parent. My life is a blend of passion, learning, and the continuous pursuit of innovation.

    Right now, I'm working at Microsoft as a Product Manager in Microsoft Security. I'm in the trenches every day, safeguarding our enterprise customers from the relentless cyber threats. I mainly focus on Enterprise Identity, building secure digital walls and constantly outthinking the 'bad actors' to stay one step ahead.

    Before Microsoft, I was part of a new team at a performance marketing company, where I was the second product hire. There, I played a pivotal role in building a mobile gaming and apps vertical from scratch - a challenging yet extremely rewarding experience.

    Going back a bit further, I put my coding skills to good use as a Front-End Engineer for Deloitte, always staying on the cutting edge of technology and design.

    At the University of Arizona’s prestigious Eller College of Management, I earned my master’s degree in management information systems. That was only the next step after completing both bachelor's and master's degrees in computer science, building a solid foundation for my future.

    When I step away from the keyboard, I step into my local community of Redmond, Washington dedicating time to volunteering and sharing joy wherever I can. I am a passionate advocate for social change, diversity, equality, and inclusion - because I firmly believe that our strength lies in our diversity.

    I hope this gives you a snapshot of who I am - both professionally and personally.
